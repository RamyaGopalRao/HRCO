Company Leave Policy

1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to outline the guidelines for employee leaves, ensuring a fair and consistent approach to time off.

2. Scope
This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of the company.

3. Types of Leave
Annual Leave

Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.

Part-time employees will receive annual leave on a pro-rata basis.

Unused leave can be carried forward to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.

A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

Parental Leave

Employees are entitled to 12 weeks of unpaid parental leave for the birth or adoption of a child.

Parental leave can be taken continuously or intermittently.

Maternity Leave

Female employees are entitled to 16 weeks of paid maternity leave.

Maternity leave can start up to 4 weeks before the expected delivery date.

Paternity Leave

Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave.

Compassionate Leave

Employees are entitled to 5 days of paid compassionate leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member.

Unpaid Leave

Employees may request unpaid leave for personal reasons.

Approval of unpaid leave is at the discretion of the management.

4. Procedure for Applying for Leave
Employees must submit a leave application form to their supervisor at least 2 weeks in advance for planned leave.

For sick leave, employees must notify their supervisor as soon as possible and provide a medical certificate if required.

Supervisors will review and approve leave requests based on operational requirements.

Approved leave will be recorded in the employee's leave balance.

5. Contact Information
For any questions or clarifications regarding the leave policy, please contact the HR department at hr@company.com.
